## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(warm acoustic guitar, soft percussion)

[Verse 1]
Clear your mind and space begins to grow
Replace complaints with words of gratitude
Stay away from those who carry bitterness
Stand close to those with clear and open eyes

[Pre-Chorus]
The heart is soil and fortune is the seed
What you plant is what you'll harvest back
One small act of kindness
Returns to you like waves upon the shore

[Chorus]
Before you earn you first must tend your luck
Before you tend your luck you tend your heart
Small offerings come back around as more
A full and peaceful heart is where blessings stay

[Verse 2]
Each night before you sleep find three things to be grateful for
Wealth will follow where a calm heart leads
Steady, precise, and ready — don't be shaken
The path will open up on its own

[Bridge]
The harder that you grasp the more it slips away
But let it go and it will find its way to you
This is the nature of money
This is the law of fortune

[Final Chorus]
Before you earn you first must tend your luck
Before you tend your luck you tend your heart
Small offerings come back around as more
A full and peaceful heart is where blessings stay

[Outro]
Seek small, embrace the struggle, look within
This is the true heart of abundance
Tend the heart, tend the luck, tend the wealth
Never change the order
(fade out)
```
