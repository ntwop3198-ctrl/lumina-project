## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(guqin and piano, flowing low)

The wise leave six questions unasked all their days
Bonds come and go — just let them find their ways
Those who don't ask — their hearts are most free

[Verse 1]
Don't ask how the bond will end
When it comes — cherish every part
When it leaves — wave and let it go
I'm glad you walked through my world — that's enough
It's not coldness — I've seen it through
Only then can I truly cherish you

[Verse 2]
Don't ask what your effort will earn
Just give this moment all you've got
Leave the outcome to heaven's will
If your heart is clear — that's enough
It's not giving up — it's letting go
Only then does freedom start to flow

[Pre-Chorus]
The more you ask, the more you cling
Those who don't ask — their minds stay clear
Six things unasked, six things released
Let go — that's when you're truly wise

[Chorus]
Six things not to ask, six things not to ask
Don't ask how the bond will end
Don't ask when they'll return from parting
Don't ask where a friend began
Don't ask what the past has been
Don't ask what comes in the next life
Six things not to ask — heart at peace

[Bridge]
Sunrise, sunset — that's one day
Spring goes, spring returns — that's one year
From child to old — that's one whole life
Ask or don't — time keeps moving on
Better to let go and follow the way

[Final Chorus]
Six things not to ask, six things not to ask
Don't ask how the bond will end
Don't ask when they'll return from parting
Don't ask where a friend began
Don't ask what the past has been
Don't ask what comes in the next life
Six things not to ask — heart at peace

[Outro]
Sunrise, sunset — that's one day
Spring goes, spring returns — that's one year
From child to old — that's one whole life
Six things not to ask — heart at peace
(fade out)
```
