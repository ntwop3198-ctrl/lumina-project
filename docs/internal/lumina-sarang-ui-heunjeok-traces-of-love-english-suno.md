## Lyrics (Suno · v3 최종 — 가사 v2 동일)

```
[Intro]
(dramatic piano, quiet start)

[Verse 1]
That narrow alley where we used to walk
Those days filled with laughter, where we belonged
Holding hands on that familiar street
Happiness bloomed with every step we'd meet
By the café window, in the golden light
Your eyes filled me completely, burning bright
When the whole world seemed to shine and glow
I never dreamed those days would end — I didn't know

[Verse 2]
Then all at once you turned so cold
One icy gaze, one single word you told
Everything collapsed in just one moment's blink
I couldn't breathe — frozen on the brink
Why did you do it — tell me, tell me why
What did I do so wrong beneath this sky
No matter how I search I can't understand
It just hurts — it hurts so much

[Verse 3]
Time flows on but nothing fades away
I try to force forget — it's here to stay
My feet may leave that road behind me now
But my heart still walks where memories burn somehow
Close my eyes and you appear in the dark
Open them — you're gone, the empty mark
The traces of our love stay carved inside
Etched in my soul for all my life
I can't erase it — I can't erase the sign
No matter how I struggle, you're still intertwined
Was this love or was this only pain
I can't tell where that line remains

[Bridge]
When the tears are done, look deep inside
There's a lamp that burns — it will not hide
No one else can ever dim that light
The strength I found to keep myself alive
Find something to do and rise again
Move my hands — let my heart follow then
On these traces I will take a brand-new stride
And soon the path will open, far and wide
Someone better is waiting there for me
Someone who will love me as I'm meant to be
As deep as these traces hurt back then
A deeper, warmer love is coming

[Outro]
My inner lamp — don't fade away
As long as I have something to do, I'm okay
A better love is on its way to me
That's why I'm still living today
(fade out)
```

## 멘토 A vs B — 판단 (기록 · v2 시절)

> **2026-06-17 제목 확정:** **Scars of Love / 사랑의 상처** 채택. 가사 속 *traces* 유지.

| 기준 | A · *Scars of Love* | B · *Traces of Love* | **우위** |
|------|---------------------|----------------------|----------|
| **제목·원意** | Scar(상처) 중심 | Trace(흔적) =  사랑의 흔적 | **B** |
| KO Verse 3 | "사랑 vs 상처"를 scar로 압축 | 흔적·love/pain 대비 유지 | **B** |
| 시적 밀도·운율 | 골목·golden light·rhyme 풍부 | 담백·짧은 행 | **A** |
| Suno 68 BPM | 행이 길어 말빨·박 밀릴 수 있음 | KO와 행 수·호흡 유사 | **B** |
| Bridge·등불 | flame 위주 | lamp / flame 혼용 | **B** (`나를 등불로` 연계) |
| Outro 화자 | I (단수) | B 초안 **we** → v2 **I**로 수정 | **B** (수정 후) |
| 철학 연계 | purpose, inner light | something to do, inner flame | **동률** |

### 결론

**전체 우위: 멘토 B (*Traces of Love*)** — 제목·원문 뼈대·Suno 호흡·루미나 **등불·할 일** 아크에 맞습니다.

**멘토 A에서 가져올 것** — Verse 1 *narrow alley / golden light*, Verse 3 *memories burn*, Bridge *brand-new stride / far and wide* (v2 통합본에 반영).

**제목 *Scars of Love*는 비권장** — 원곡이 묻는 질문은 *「사랑이었는지 상처였는지」* 이지, 처음부터 상처로 정의하지 않습니다.
