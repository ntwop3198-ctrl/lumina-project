## Lyrics (Suno · v2 정본)

```
[Intro]
(Soft acoustic guitar, a quiet chuckle)

[Verse 1]
Lost my shoes, wandered around for a while
Thought I'd meet a fairy, turned out to be an old man
He found them for me, said just go barefoot then
I apologized, got a zen riddle in return

[Verse 2]
Drunk in high school, woke up so thirsty
Had no choice but to sip from the mop bucket
It wasn't a magic potion, just the mop bucket water
Nothing special about it, didn't even get sick

[Chorus]
Just like that, yet not quite just like that
Just like that, seems like a big deal yet not at all
Whatever, whatever, whatever, if it's there it's there
If it's not then forget it, just like that

[Verse 3]
Money money money, everyone chasing it
Almost everyone in this world eyes on the cash
Eighteen years of grievance, eighteen years of bitterness
Looking back now, just laughing just like that

[Bridge]
Thirsty so drink, hungry so eat
Sleepy so sleep, having leads to wanting
Not even a bowl, empty is what holds
What we let go by force stays with us deepest

[Final Chorus]
True emptiness, wondrous existence, nothing yet not nothing
Just like that is one, yet not only one
The moment you try to do it right, you're already doing it
Just like that, just like that, just like that, just like that

[Outro]
(Guitar slowly fading)
Just like that——
```
