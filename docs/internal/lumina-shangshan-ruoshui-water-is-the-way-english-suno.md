## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(water sounds, guqin, piano low)

Reversal is the movement of the Tao
Yielding is the way the Tao works
All things under heaven are born from being
Being is born from nothing

[Verse 1]
It's not weakness — it's the soft that overcomes the hard
It's not retreat — it's stepping back to move forward
Water flows to the lowest place, nourishing all things in silence
Yet carves through the hardest stone
The harder you force, the tighter you grip
The further you drift from the answer

[Verse 2]
Every answer hides on the opposite side
Fall to the deepest valley to reach the peak no one else can climb
Where you are weakest — that's where strength is born
What rises must fall — learn to leave some space

[Pre-Chorus]
Don't try so hard
Flow with heaven and earth's breath
Give yourself to the Tao's current
Go where your heart leads — free and light

[Chorus]
The highest good is like water
Water serves all things without competing
Reversal is the movement of the Tao
Yielding is the way the Tao works
Follow the flow — stay human
Go against it — become something more
The answer is always on the other side

[Bridge]
The lowest point is where power gathers
The higher the peak you're meant to climb
The deeper the valley you must pass through
Like tide and ebb — the course was set before
Let go of what you're holding
Follow the Tao — flow freely

[Final Chorus]
The highest good is like water
Water serves all things without competing
Reversal is the movement of the Tao
Yielding is the way the Tao works
Follow the flow — stay human
Go against it — become something more
The answer is always on the other side

[Outro]
Reversal is the movement of the Tao
Yielding is the way the Tao works
Like water — just flow — the answer is on the other side
(fade out)
```
