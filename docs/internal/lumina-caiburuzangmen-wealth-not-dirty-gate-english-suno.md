## Lyrics (Suno · v1 final)

```
[Intro]
(soft piano, gentle guzheng)

[Verse 1]
How long did it take to open this door
Wiping away the grime others left behind
Scrubbing until my hands wore down
With clean hands I hung a brand new door
What comes easy disappears just as easy
Only what is built the right way lasts
Now through this door good fortune enters
Wealth does not enter a dirty gate — now I understand

[Verse 2]
Wealth will not come to a dirty heart
Heaven turns its back on deceit and greed
It may look brilliant but inside it is empty
The faster you climb the harder you fall
Be grateful for small things and walk the right path
Heaven never truly looks away
Chasing shortcuts only hurts yourself in the end
Slow is fine — the righteous path is the real one

[Chorus]
Wealth does not enter a dirty gate
A dirty door cannot hold good fortune
Open this door
With a clean heart
What greed builds is a house on sand
What integrity builds is a house on rock
Heaven is watching
Before a righteous gate good fortune naturally stays

[Verse 3]
A seat built by stepping on others is never secure
What is gained through deception becomes poison
But from the very place where you fell
The one who rises again becomes stronger
The pain you walked through becomes wisdom
The time you endured becomes fortune
Now I know — before a righteous gate
One day the light will surely come

[Bridge - Philosophy]
Seek the small
Penny by penny walk the righteous path
Seek the hardship
That suffering purifies the heart
Seek within yourself
A clean heart is where good fortune comes
Wealth does not enter a dirty gate
Before a righteous gate good fortune naturally stays

[Final Chorus]
Wealth does not enter a dirty gate
A dirty door cannot hold good fortune
Open this door
With a clean heart
What greed builds is a house on sand
What integrity builds is a house on rock
Heaven is watching
Before a righteous gate good fortune naturally stays

[Outro]
Today I hung a brand new door
The door I scrubbed clean with my own hands
Wealth does not enter a dirty gate
Through this door the light comes in 🙏
(fade out)
```
