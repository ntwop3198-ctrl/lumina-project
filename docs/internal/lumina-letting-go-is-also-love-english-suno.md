# Letting Go Is Also Love (舍得 · English Pop Ballad)

> **放手也是爱** · Gemini 에디팅 (음절·라임·서구 팝 발라드) · Cursor 정리  
> Suno 스태프 추천곡 톤 · **English primary** · Outro `舍得` 뿌리 표식

---

## Title (Suno 제목란)

```
Letting Go Is Also Love (舍得 English Version)
```

## Style (Suno 스타일란 — 팝 발라드 최적화)

```
Acoustic folk pop ballad, fingerpicking guitar, warm emotional male vocal,
melancholic indie pop, slow tempo 72bpm, minimal sad strings, cello,
intimate, bittersweet, reflective cinematic atmosphere, not epic,
English with whispered Chinese outro
```

## Lyrics (Suno 가사란 — 붙여넣기용)

```
[Intro]
Let go...
Even when it breaks my heart.
Letting go is love.

[Verse 1]
When there's still warmth inside our hands,
When there's still something left to feel.
Before the anger takes the place,
Of every wound we tried to heal.
I'd rather say goodbye with grace,
Before we cross the line of hate.
This is the final open door,
To save us from a darker fate.

[Chorus]
Holding on was love,
But letting go is love too.
It takes more strength to set you free,
Than holding tight to you.
Before we turn to enemies,
Before the tears turn into walls.
I'll let you go with what remains,
Of the love that built it all.

[Verse 2]
Thank you for the years we walked,
Thank you for our daughter's smile.
I couldn't give you everything,
But you made it all worthwhile.
No hidden shame, no bitter words,
I bury them deep in the past.
We're choosing peace over the pain,
To make the good things last.

[Bridge]
It's not that love has disappeared,
It just knows when it's time to leave.
The kindest thing that we can do,
Is to unravel what we weave.

[Final Chorus]
Holding on was love,
But letting go is love too.
It takes more strength to set you free,
Than holding tight to you.
Before we turn to enemies,
Before the tears turn into walls.
I'll let you go with what remains,
Of the love that built it all.

[Outro]
舍得
I'll let you go.
This is my last love for you.
Take care.
Stay well.
```

## 에디팅 포인트 (Gemini)

### 보컬 싱크 · 라임

| 쌍 | 역할 |
|----|------|
| hands / feel | Verse 1 — 기타 박에 맞춘 음절 |
| place / heal | 상처·분노 전환 |
| grace / hate | **원수 되기 전** 고결한 작별 |
| free / you · walls / all | Chorus — 팝 훅 |

### 서사 승화

- **Verse 2:** `No hidden shame, no bitter words, I bury them deep in the past` — 잠재의식 **수치심**·쓰라린 말을 과거에 묻음 (11일·장인 면담과 연결).
- **Chorus:** `Before we turn to enemies` — **발목 놓기** = 원수 되기 전 선택.
- **Outro:** `舍得` — 완전 영어곡 속 **뿌리 낙인** (읊조·whisper — Style에 반영).

### Claude 초안 대비

| 항목 | 변경 |
|------|------|
| Style | 어쿠스틱 fingerpicking + **cello** + indie pop |
| Chorus | Holding on **was** love (과거형 — 이미 놓는 중) |
| Verse 2 | 수치·평화 선택 문장 **추가** |
| Outro | 舍得 + Take care / Stay well |
