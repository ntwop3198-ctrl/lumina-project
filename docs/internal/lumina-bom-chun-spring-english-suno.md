## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

[Intro]
(quiet strings, spring breeze)

[Verse 1]
Through the long and weary waiting
Cold and frozen winter passed
New life stirs within the silence
Spring has come at last

[Verse 2]
Spring has come upon the earth
In the colors women wear
In the gentle breeze that blows
Spring is everywhere

[Chorus]
Walk the path your bright eyes show you
Just keep walking on

Winter's frozen chill

Won't let me walk with will

Spring will come again
Deep within my frozen heart
Spring will melt the pain

[Final Chorus]
Walk the path your bright eyes show you
Just keep walking on
Where the flowers bloom and hope awaits
That itself is spring

[Outro]
That itself is spring
That itself is spring
(fade out)
