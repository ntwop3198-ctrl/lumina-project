## Lyrics (Suno · v1 final)

```
[Intro]
(guzheng and gayageum, the breath of dawn wind)

[Verse 1]
The moment Dongmin walked into Mount Taibai
he said he had become the mountain itself
so did the mountain, too, become Dongmin
where does the line between man and mountain lie

[Verse 2]
When you smile, I smile too
when you weep, my heart grows wet
my joy has always been your joy
your pain has always been my pain

[Chorus]
I am you, you are me
you are me, I am you
the earth is me, I am the earth
no boundary — all things are one
the one is the many, the many is the one
one is the whole, the whole is one

[Verse 3]
Seek little, seek hardship, seek yourself
in saving myself
I caught sight of you
in saving you
I caught sight of myself
true emptiness holds wondrous being
because it is empty, we can hold each other

[Chorus]
I am you, you are me
you are me, I am you
the earth is me, I am the earth
no boundary — all things are one
the one is the many, the many is the one
one is the whole, the whole is one

[Bridge]
the mountain is still the mountain
Dongmin is still Dongmin
I am still I
you are still you
the mountain is still the mountain
yet in the end, all was always one
even I myself
never knew

[Instrumental Break]
(guzheng solo, silence between notes)

[Final Chorus]
I am you, you are me
you are me, I am you
the earth is me, I am the earth
no boundary — all things are one
the one is the many, the many is the one
one is the whole, the whole is one

[Outro]
I once was you
you once were me
the earth was once us
the mountain is still the mountain
you are me
(fade out)
```
