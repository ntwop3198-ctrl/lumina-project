## Lyrics (Suno · v1)

```
[Intro]
(warm acoustic guitar)

[Verse 1]
Your tiny hands gripped the edge of the table
Your little legs trembling as you stood
You called out — Daddy
That day felt like winning the whole world

Everyone else calls for Mama first
But you called for Daddy
My princess, do you know
You were always such a special child

[Chorus]
Daddy's heart was so full
Even after twenty years
To Daddy it feels like yesterday
The memory is still so clear

[Verse 2]
Up on the slide
You shone like the morning sun just rising
That brilliant smile of yours
Still lives right here before my eyes

When you came home late
Daddy worried so much for you
Waiting from a distance
Eyes never leaving your shadow
Until I heard your footsteps coming home

[Chorus]
Daddy thought even your little messes were precious
That Daddy is still right here waiting
Those tiny toddling feet of yours
Lit up Daddy's entire world

[Bridge]
I miss you my princess
Watching you quietly from afar
The love Daddy has for you
Will never change — like the sun

[Final Chorus]
One day Daddy will take your hand
And walk with you down a bright open road
Daddy can wait patiently
I miss you my princess
You will always live in Daddy's heart

[Outro]
(guitar slowly fading)
Daddy can wait patiently
I miss you my princess
```
