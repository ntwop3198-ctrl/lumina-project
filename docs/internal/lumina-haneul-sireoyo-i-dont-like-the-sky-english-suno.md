## Lyrics (Suno · v1 final)

```
[Intro]
(soft guitar arpeggio, warm cello)

[Verse 1]
Everyone who loves the sky has flown up high
I'm just staying here down below
What's so great about climbing that high
I know exactly how much it hurts to fall
The higher an empty barrel climbs
The louder and harder it crashes down

[Verse 2]
I've fallen before — fallen hard and big
Only after hitting bottom did I learn the ground is better
The sky is just borrowed speed
This ground beneath my feet is the real thing
You have to look down to pick something up
Stare at the sky and you'll just go hungry

[Chorus]
I don't like the sky — falling hurts too much
You have to look at the ground to find anything worth keeping
A high-climbing empty barrel crashes loud
I just like the basement
Rent is cheap and it's nice and quiet
Roots grow deeper going down not up

[Verse 3]
There were nights I was driven out into the cold wind
But that freezing night became my root
Because I was hurt I became stronger
Because I fell I learned how to walk
Because my heart was empty the light came in
That's not something to fear

[Bridge - Philosophy]
Seek the small — the little things are enough
One ray of light one smile is enough
Seek hardship — because I hurt I now understand
The suffering made me deeper
Seek within — the answer lives inside
I found it in the basement
True emptiness holds everything
Nothing yet not nothing

[Final Chorus]
I don't like the sky — falling hurts too much
You have to look at the ground to find anything worth keeping
A high-climbing empty barrel crashes loud
I just like the basement
Empty — only then can you truly be full
The lowest place is the most solid place of all

[Outro]
I don't like the sky
Falling hurts too much — ha
I just like the basement
The rent is cheap too — ha ha
Sitting with the Bodhisattva over a cup of tea is enough
(fade out)
```
