## Lyrics (Suno · v1 final)

```
[Intro]
(soft piano, gentle acoustic guitar)

[Verse 1]
Even if you have a thousand friends
One who stays till the end is enough
Not the fancy words but that quiet gaze
That one person was everything

The ones who gathered when things were good
Disappeared one by one when times got hard
But the one who stayed right there
That's a real friend

[Verse 2]
The higher you climb the fewer words
The better things get the more distant they feel
But still unchanged in that same place
My friend, thank you

Sharing just one meal together
Saying nothing just smiling
Looking back that was the best of times
That person was the most precious of all

[Chorus]
A friend isn't about cheering you on
It's just being there
Knowing without being told
Staying and never leaving
A friend isn't made of wood and stone
It's growing together as each other's strength
One friend like that in this life
That is enough

[Verse 3]
There was a thousand square foot office
And a fifty square foot rented room
Through every season unchanging
That face comes back to me
Now each of us in our own place
Walking quietly down our own road
But that smile I think of sometimes
That is my strength

[Bridge - Philosophy]
Seek the small
Seek the hardship
Seek within yourself
Because you were there I could keep walking
A friend grows with you and lifts you as they grow
It doesn't have to be grand
It doesn't matter if we're far apart
Just knowing you're there
Is enough for me

[Final Chorus]
A friend isn't about cheering you on
It's just being there
Knowing without being told
Staying and never leaving
A friend isn't made of wood and stone
It's growing together as each other's strength
One friend like that in this life
That is enough

[Outro]
One friend in this life
That is enough
My friend, thank you 🙏
(fade out)
```
