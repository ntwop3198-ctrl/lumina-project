## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(guqin and piano, slow and low)

No Bodhi tree was ever there
No mirror and no stand
Nothing was here from the start
So where could dust land

[Verse 1]
No temple visits, no bowing to statues
Buddha lives inside, not in form
A mountain trail at dawn is my temple
Every step beneath my feet is how I'm born
Creating music is my dharma gate
One line of lyrics can reveal the truth within

[Verse 2]
Huineng was just a man who cut wood
Couldn't read, yet saw his nature first
Let go of clinging, let go of judgment
Don't ask where you came from or where you'll go
Nothing to find when there's no thirst
This moment right here — this is the place

[Pre-Chorus]
I searched outside my whole life
And came back empty-handed
I turned around and saw myself
I never left to begin with

[Chorus]
No Bodhi tree was ever there
No need to search outside
No mirror and no stand to wipe
Nothing left to hide
Nothing here from the very start
Let go and you'll be free
Where could the dust ever settle
When dust was never meant to be

[Bridge]
Buddha lives within your nature
Don't seek him far away
Be your own lamp, your own light
Don't lean on others' rays
Let the wind come as it comes
Let the clouds go as they go
Your true nature never moved
Only the mind was moving all along

[Final Chorus]
No Bodhi tree was ever there
No need to search outside
No mirror and no stand to wipe
Nothing left to hide
Nothing here from the very start
Let go and you'll be free
Where could the dust ever settle
When dust was never meant to be

[Outro]
Nothing was here from the start
So where could dust ever land
(fade out)
```
