## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(acoustic guitar, quiet start)

[Verse 1]
Say you know — they come at you with envy
Say you don't — they rush to teach you something
Pretend to know and fumble long enough — you might learn
But saying "I don't know" — that's the real answer
The less you know, the wider grows your inner world
The more you know, the smaller gets your head
Learning and living — an ocean with no end
The more you don't know, the more alive you are

[Verse 2]
Just as the body lets go of waste and sweat
Let stale thoughts and heavy feelings drift away
The deep truth your body already knew and whispered
Your mind just takes a little longer to catch up
The harder you grasp, the more it slips like sand
Let it go — and somehow it comes closer still
Don't force your way to understanding
When the time is right, it blooms like a flower

[Chorus]
Pretending to know what you don't
Pretending not to know what you do
Somewhere in that blurry boundary lies the middle way
The courage to say "I don't know" — this life's only answer

[Bridge]
Search a thick book — the answer isn't there
The answer comes beside a steaming bowl of rice
Sink deep in thought and the road ahead gets blocked
Hum a little tune and it all melts away
Right where the mind gave up and let it go
The heart sits quietly and smiles at you
The day I truly knew that I knew nothing at all
Was the most peaceful day of my life

[Final Chorus]
Pretending to know what you don't
Pretending not to know what you do
Somewhere in that blurry boundary lies the middle way
The courage to say "I don't know" — this life's only answer

[Outro]
Just like that — just not knowing
Just like that — just letting it flow
Leave it be and it flows on its own
(fade out)
```
