## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(gentle piano and strings, Bodhi leaves in wind)

Sitting alone beneath the Bodhi tree
Closing my eyes, I start to see
No wind, no sound, no stir at all
In that still place, I see it all

[Verse 1]
In the darkness called ignorance
Volition stirs, consciousness wakes
Taking shape as name and form
Six senses touch the world
With eyes and ears, with nose and tongue
The body feels, the mind thinks on
Every link in the chain of cause
All of this connects without pause

[Verse 2]
Contact touches — feeling rises
Craving burns, clinging grows
Becoming is, and birth comes on
Old age and death can't be undone
The harder you grasp, the more it slips away
The harder you push, the more it stays
Tracing the chain to find its end
No beginning found — no end

[Chorus]
Everything came by dependent arising
By dependent arising it will return
Let go of clinging — suffering disappears
In the empty place, freedom appears

[Bridge]
When ignorance fades, volition will stop
When volition stops, consciousness grows still
One by one the flames go out
That is liberation
Don't force the flame to go out
When conditions end, it fades on its own
Without seeking — when the time is due
In the empty place, quiet comes through

[Final Chorus]
Everything came by dependent arising
By dependent arising it will return
Let go of clinging — suffering disappears
In the empty place, freedom appears

[Outro]
Beneath the Bodhi tree the wind blows free
One petal falls so quietly
Coming and going — that is life
Gone and back again — the same life
(fade out)
```
