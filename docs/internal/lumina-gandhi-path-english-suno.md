## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(low acoustic guitar, hot wind of India)

Beneath the burning sun of India
He sat turning the spinning wheel
He sought no power, no fame
In silence — he changed the world

[Verse 1]
He never raised a gun
He never raised a fist
Walking barefoot on the salt road
Millions followed in his steps
He never used fear as a weapon
He met hatred with love
Practicing every small thing himself
That path became a revolution

[Verse 2]
He fasted against injustice
Stood unbroken before bayonets
With one frail body he shook the world
And brought an empire to its knees
What is won by the sword is lost by the sword
But what is won by truth lasts forever
By the power of Satyagraha — truth force
He lifted the oppressed to their feet

[Chorus]
He wanted no honor, no wealth
He covered himself with one piece of plain cloth
That simplicity, that devotion, that stubborn practice
Will shine on through a thousand years

[Bridge]
Be the change you wish to see in the world
He turned the wheel himself, he made the salt himself
He showed it not in words but in life
Violence only breeds more violence
Forgiveness is the strongest weapon, he said
The world laughed — he did not waver
He stood firm on the ground of truth

[Final Chorus]
He wanted no honor, no wealth
He covered himself with one piece of plain cloth
That simplicity, that devotion, that stubborn practice
Will shine on through a thousand years

[Outro]
The evening sky of India glows red
The sound of the spinning wheel carried on the wind
The seed of nonviolence scattered through the world
Those great empty hands, that empty bowl
Still living in someone's heart today
(fade out)
```
