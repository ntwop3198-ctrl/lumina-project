## Lyrics (Suno · v1 final)

```
[Intro]
(low, as if counting stars)
There's a river flowing across the sky
They call it the Milky Way

[Verse 1]
A young man who herded cattle
A young woman who wove silk
They fell in love and forgot their work
So the heavens grew angry and tore them apart
One at the eastern edge, one at the west
Facing each other, yet never touching
Longing became a river between them
Flowing on, unbroken, for a thousand years

[Pre-Chorus]
Just one day in a whole year
Magpies gather to build a bridge
So their hands can finally meet
Even the heavens soften their heart

[Chorus]
On the seventh night of the seventh moon
I cross the Milky Way to you
Carrying all the words left unsaid
I walked a whole year just for this
Even apart, love still remains
Longing itself became the bridge
Cowherd and Weaver, this old, old story
Lives on inside every heart

[Verse 2]
Where did this story first begin?
Under whose sky did it first take shape?
It crossed the mountains, crossed the seas
Reborn each time in a different tongue
Some carved it into the mountainside
Some planted it deep in a village
Some etched it into stone
But it all came from the very same star

[Pre-Chorus]
Just one day in a whole year
Magpies gather to build a bridge
So their hands can finally meet

[Chorus]

I cross the Milky Way to you

Longing itself became the bridge

[Bridge]
求小求苦求己
seek small, seek hardship, seek yourself
```
