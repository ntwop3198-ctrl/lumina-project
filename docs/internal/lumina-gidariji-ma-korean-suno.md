## Style (Suno 스타일란)

```
Korean acoustic folk ballad, emotional male vocal,
sparse acoustic guitar opening, gentle strings building through chorus,
heartfelt and urgent, talk-singing style in verses,
not epic, not trot, 80~90bpm, fade out ending
```
