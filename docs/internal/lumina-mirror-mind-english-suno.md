## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(gentle water, guqin, distant temple bell)

[Verse 1]
I walk along the ridge of that open field
The lotus pond is white and clear
Fish and catfish rise from these waters
Birds and creatures find their home here

[Pre-Chorus]
Wisdom grows together with practice
A deep intention makes words turn kind
A sincere heart brings peaceful dreams
Heaven's truth opens on this path

[Chorus]
To make the virtue of the heart complete
First awaken to the way of heaven
My mind is a bright and clear mirror
Dust may fall but cannot leave a stain
The rocky stream roars like distant thunder
The garden tree is dreaming of spring
Still I walk this path today
Knowing that the heart itself is heaven

[Bridge]
Heaven listens in silence without sound
Blue and vast — where do you search for it
It is not high and it is not far
It lives only within the human heart

[Outro]
My mind is a clear mirror
Reflecting dust yet never stained
The heart becomes a mirror
Lighting up this world
(fade out)
```
