## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(quiet water, distant temple bell)

Who drew the line
between friend and foe

[Verse 1]
Hold hot charcoal — your hand burns first
The flame of hate begins inside of me
The higher I stack the wall called me
The colder the loneliness that knocks

[Verse 2]
The Buddha said — don't pick the fight
Poison not received returns to its source
Jesus said — love, but even that love
Reaches the place where all are one

[Chorus]
We were never two — so what is there to hate
A shadow only lives where there is light
When even the word enemy melts away
Compassion starts to move through me
Hate will never wash away with hate
Letting go of me — the greatest love there is

[Bridge]
Empty, yet full — the truth that fills when cleared
Behind the name I called my enemy
Another face of mine looks back at me
As in a mirror — as we look at each other
We were always shards of the same light

[Final Chorus]
We were never two — so what is there to hate
A shadow only lives where there is light
When even the word enemy melts away
Compassion starts to move through me
Hate will never wash away with hate
Letting go of me — the greatest love there is

[Outro]
One shadow, one breath
Hatred fades away
Only peace remains
(fade out)
```
