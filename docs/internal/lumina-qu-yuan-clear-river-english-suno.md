## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(guqin low and slow, cello like cold river flowing)

[Verse 1]
The whole world muddied — I alone stay clear
Everyone drunk — I alone stay awake
Who could understand this cold loneliness of clarity
Only the Miluo River receives my ancient tears
The higher I climb the more alone I become
The clearer I am the more I walk alone
Has the world grown murky
Or was I simply too clear

[Verse 2]
The fisherman asked — why hold on so stubbornly
Go with the flow and you won't be lonely
I know this well — but I feared my body would be stained
Better to give my purity to this rushing river
In a world where bending means living
He chose to stand until the very end
Never victorious — yet never defeated
That name has echoed through a thousand years

[Chorus]
If he'd only bowed his head a little — less pain
If he'd only closed his eyes a little — he could have smiled
The one who lost because he was too clear
The one who became a river because he was too awake
Qu Yuan — you faced the tide of your age alone

[Bridge]
The world never stopped pointing fingers at me
But the mirror inside me never broke
Not compromising — that was my only power
Not kneeling — that was my last dignity
Losing is not always truly losing
Bending is not always truly living
That stubbornness, that clarity, that loneliness
Outlived a thousand years

[Final Chorus]
If he'd only bowed his head a little — less pain
If he'd only closed his eyes a little — he could have smiled
The one who lost because he was too clear
The one who became a river because he was too awake
Qu Yuan — you faced the tide of your age alone

[Outro]
A thousand years have passed — the river flows on quietly
Each Duanwu we cast rice dumplings to soothe your spirit
You who stayed clear alone — now rest as the river
That heart too clear and too lonely
Still lives in someone's chest today
(fade out)
```
