## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(soft electric piano, muted guitar, spacious ambiance)

[Verse 1]
Loving someone doesn't mean you'll always wait
The one who waits just loves a little deeper
I know I should forget
But your face still lives inside this middle-aged heart

[Verse 2]
I kept on knocking at a door that wouldn't open
I knew all along that wasn't right
Taking one step back
Isn't losing — it's dignity

[Chorus]
When the passion fades I'll quietly walk away
I won't be knocking at that door anymore
I surrender to the old story now

[Bridge]

There won't be anyone quite like me
What comes, I meet — what goes, I release
That's who I am, that's who I am

[Final Chorus]
When the passion fades I'll quietly walk away
I won't be knocking at that door anymore
I surrender to the old story now
Because a new one has to begin

[Outro]
I've learned the way to close a door
That is love's final dignity
(fade out)
```
