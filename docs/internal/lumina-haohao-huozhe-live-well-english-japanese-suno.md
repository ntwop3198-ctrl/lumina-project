## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(soft guitar arpeggio, gentle strings)

[Verse 1 - English]
Eat well, sleep well
That's the smallest and hardest thing about living
No need for grand meaning
No need for a perfect answer
The warmth of a bowl of porridge
Is all that today needs to be

[Verse 1 - 日本語]
ちゃんと食べて ちゃんと眠って
それが生きることの一番小さくて難しいこと
大きな意味なんていらない
完璧な答えも要らない
一杯のお粥のぬくもり
それが今日のすべて

[Chorus - EN·JP Mix]
Life is just layers of experience piled on top of each other
Bitter or sweet — they're all pieces of your story
Don't drain yourself — let everything drift away
人は去りお茶は冷める — still, we're here like this, alive

[Verse 2]
Don't be tied to yesterday
Don't borrow from tomorrow before it comes
Like the dawn sky flowing outside the window
Our day will brighten just like that
明日のことを心配しすぎないで
あの少しずつ明るくなる空を見て

[Bridge - 철학]
Seek the small — the little things are enough
One ray of light, one small smile will do
Seek hardship — even pain is just passing through
Rain must fall before the earth grows strong
Seek within — come back to yourself
Find the peace that lives inside your heart
Live well
That simple promise is the answer

[Final Chorus]
Life is just layers of experience piled on top of each other
Bitter or sweet — they're all yours to keep
Don't drain yourself — just let it flow
人は去りお茶は冷める — still, we're here, beautifully alive

[Outro]
Live well
生きていこう
Today, one warm bowl of rice
That's enough
(fade out)
```
