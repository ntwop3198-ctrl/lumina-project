## Lyrics (Suno · v1 final)

```
[Intro]
(solo guzheng, ocean waves slowly rising)

[Verse 1]
On Meizhou Island, a daughter of the Lin family
Born without a cry, they named her Mo Niang
At sixteen she came to read the sky and sea
She crossed the waves to save others, never going home

[Verse 2]
Her father and brother sailed into a storm
Mo Niang closed her eyes, her spirit took flight
One hand held her father, one hand held her brother
Her mother's single cry — and the brother was already gone

[Chorus]
Mazu, oh Mazu, light over the sea
A thousand years unfading, lighting the way home
In every fisherman's heart, the eternal mother
However wild the storm, there's still a direction

[Verse 3]
At twenty-eight she rose and became light
On Meifeng's peak, white robes trailing in the wind
From that day on the fishing boats were no longer alone
Before her shrine, the incense never stops burning

[Verse 4]
A thousand sails crossing ten thousand miles
Faith in Mazu followed the boats across the sea
Taiwan and Meizhou, one root of incense and flame
One thread of clear smoke links both shores together

[Interlude]
(solo erhu, distant and long)

[Bridge]
Not a myth, but a woman
Who gave herself away, and carried thousands across
The road of seeking small, seeking pain, seeking self
She walked it first, a thousand years ago
Seek small — even one fishing light is still light
Seek pain — she never once hid from the storm
Seek self — her heart held only others' peace
This road, she walked it for us, long before we did

[Chorus]
Mazu, oh Mazu, light over the sea
A thousand years unfading, lighting the way home
In every fisherman's heart, the eternal mother
However wild the storm, there's still a direction

[Outro]
Mo Niang, Mo Niang, became light
Lighting the way home for all who return
A thousand years later, the lantern still burns
Waiting for every ship to come home safe
(fade out)
```
