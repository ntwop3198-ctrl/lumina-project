[Intro]
(sparse acoustic guitar, quiet)

[Verse 1]
I didn't know — I just didn't know
The words "I love you" melted on my tongue
Before they ever left my lips
My father held my hand and taught me well
That holding silence was the mark of a man
But nobody ever told me
Love was the one thing that needed a voice

[Chorus]
Like autumn leaves in a bitter wind
Our love falls away
One cold breath and they fall soft and thin
No fight, no cry — just dropping to the ground
To that tender, beautiful face
I only handed thorns
And I'm drowning in regret

[Verse 2]
I always thought I stood above you somehow
Too proud to say you're lovely, thank you, well done
Such small words — why did I hold them back so long
The cruel ones landed deep and never let you go
I stand before the mirror now
Ashamed down to the bone, my chest gone cold and bare

[Chorus]
Like autumn leaves in a bitter wind
Our love falls away
One cold breath and they fall soft and thin
No fight, no cry — just dropping to the ground
To that tender, beautiful face
I only handed thorns
And I'm drowning in regret

[Bridge]
I thought keeping it inside would be enough
That silence was the same as love
I was a fool to think the heart could speak
Without a voice to carry it across

[Outro]
Like morning pulls the sun up from the line
My heart never once strayed — that much was true
This old fool is finally saying it out loud
I'm sorry
I truly am
(voice fades to end)
