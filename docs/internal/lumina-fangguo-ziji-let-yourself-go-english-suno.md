## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(piano fades in, strings slowly rise)

No one ever put those chains on you
When you've walked it to the end
Just let yourself go

[Verse 1]
The money in your pocket fades a little every day
And the hourglass of life grows shorter too
You pull out an old photograph
And the face that used to smile has blurred away
You lived for children, pride, for everyone around
But somewhere lost the one you started as

[Verse 2]
You held on for a lifetime, grabbed it tight
And ended up with nothing but a fist of sand
We all forgot somewhere along the way
How to bloom a single flower just for ourselves
That road you've walked a thousand times before
You walk it now alone — and you're okay

[Pre-Chorus]
You stand before the mirror, can't recognize your face
Those tears you cry — tell me, who were they for
When the bond has run its course, just walk away with grace
Stop fighting with the shadows of the past

[Chorus]
Let yourself go, let yourself go
Welcome what comes, release what leaves
Give back to others what was always theirs
Give back to yourself the self you lost so long ago

[Bridge]
We live inside the landscape of the mind
Not inside the world we see
Let flowers bloom and let the trees grow tall
Walk this road a while — it's okay to part in peace
The wind blows through and sounds like someone's voice
But you don't have to turn around anymore

[Final Chorus]
Let yourself go, let yourself go
Welcome what comes, release what leaves
Give back to others what was always theirs
Give back to yourself the self you lost so long ago

[Outro]
Listen to the wind — no more sighing now
Let yourself go
Just give yourself back to yourself
(fade out)
```
