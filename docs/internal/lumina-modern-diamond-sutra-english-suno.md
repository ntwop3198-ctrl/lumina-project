## Lyrics (Suno · v2)

```
[Intro]
(soft acoustic guitar, calm and still)

[Verse 1]
The judging mind sticks like rice cake
Won't let go, won't peel away
This is right, that is wrong
Too afraid to lose if I let go

[Verse 2]
Did Bodhidharma just stare at the wall?
The wall is a wall, yet not a wall
The Buddha sat under a tree
Who would sit under the blazing sun?

[Verse 3]
If there's a pine tree, it's a pine tree
If there's a willow tree, it's a willow tree
Calling it a Bodhi tree
Does it take a Bodhi tree to be enlightened?

[Chorus]
Let it go, let it go
Let go of that judging mind
Empty to receive
Release to be free

[Bridge]
Seek the small, seek the hard, seek yourself
Seek humility in little things
Seek growth through hardship
Seek to know who you are

Two thousand five hundred years ago or now
Truth is one
Point directly at the mind
See your own true face

[Final Chorus]
Let it go, let it go
Let go of the fear of losing
Let it go, let it go
Empty and the universe fills you

[Outro]
Even letting go, love remains
Step back and the sea opens, the sky expands
Empty and the universe fills you
(fade out)
```
