# 38선 (三八線 · 38th Parallel) — Cyberpunk Break

> **A실험** · 「그 겨울, 38선」folk ballad의 **사면팔방 자매곡**  
> 역사의 상처 × 사이버펑크 · Suno Short + Claude Full 참고

## Style

```
Cyberpunk EDM, Guzheng, Future bass, 130bpm, dystopian, cold synth, Korean-Chinese fusion, trilingual, haunting male vocal, electric tension, dark atmospheric, haegeum accent, not epic
```
