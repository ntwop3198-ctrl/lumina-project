## Lyrics (Suno · v1 final)

```
[Intro]
(soft guitar arpeggio, gentle strings)

[Verse 1]
When the elevator opens everyone rushes to the brightest floor
Someone today is only going up again
But if the power cuts out you're trapped inside
The higher an empty barrel climbs
The louder and harder it falls

[Verse 2]
I just like the basement — it's quiet down here
And the rent is cheap too — ha
An empty bowl is different — light can come in
I'm empty too and that's not something to fear
A shell forced on by pressure always falls away
The truly solid place is in the quiet basement

[Chorus]
I like the basement, I like the basement
Roots don't grow toward the sky — they go down deep
Light doesn't only come from above
It rises quietly from somewhere inside
True emptiness holds everything — nothing yet not nothing

[Verse 3]
The stairs are slow but my feet are on the ground
The elevator is just borrowed speed
Even the night I was driven out can become my root
It's at the lowest point that the earth grows strong

[Bridge - Philosophy]
Seek the small — one step at a time down here
Even the basement has its own order and way
Seek hardship — the nights I was driven out
Those are the roots that make me stronger every day
Seek within — the answer lives inside
The Bodhisattva stays at the lowest place too
I just like the basement

[Final Chorus]
I like the basement, I like the basement
Roots don't grow toward the sky — they go down deep
Light doesn't only come from above
It rises quietly from somewhere inside
Empty — only then can you truly be full

[Outro]
While others go up I go down
In the deepest place I brew a fresh pot of tea
I just like the basement
The rent is cheap too — ha
(fade out)
```
