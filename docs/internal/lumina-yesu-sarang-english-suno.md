## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(gentle piano and acoustic guitar, Galilee waves)

Walking barefoot along the shores of Galilee
No home to call his own — yet he smiled so bright
He sought no power, no wealth, no earthly gain
Owning nothing — he treasured every soul

[Verse 1]
He went to the poor
He took the hand of the sick
Love your neighbor as yourself
Those words spread through the whole world
He turned no one away — tax collector, outcast
He sat at the table with the sinner
The high shall be made low, the low shall be raised
That teaching lives on today

[Verse 2]
Love even your enemies, he said
He sat beside the rejected and forgotten
Even with faith as small as a mustard seed
Mountains can be moved, he said
He ran to meet the returning prodigal son
He went out to find the one lost sheep
One soul more precious than a hundred
That love lives within us still

[Chorus]
He never once desired a roof of his own
In that emptied heart he held the love of all the world
That devotion, that love, that living it out
Will shine on through a thousand years

[Bridge]
Even on the cross
Father, forgive them
With nothing that could pierce the sky
He held the whole world in his arms
A love that even death could not stop
Rising again on the third day
He came to find his trembling disciples
Peace be with you, he said

[Final Chorus]
He never once desired a roof of his own
In that emptied heart he held the love of all the world
That devotion, that love, that living it out
Will shine on through a thousand years

[Outro]
The wind off Galilee still blows today
His footsteps carved into our hearts
Love, forgive, be humble
The one who fills the empty bowl with love
Those words still ring out today
(fade out)
```
