## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(piano soft, slowly rising)

I don't need you to love me
I just need to bloom
Live myself into a flower
And the fragrance finds its way

[Verse 1]
The more you love someone, the more you need to keep yourself
The more you fear losing, the easier it slips away
When we're together — play and enjoy it fully
When we're apart — let each other be
Nothing can shake me — the rhythm stays right here in my hands

[Verse 2]
It's not against human nature — it's turning inward
It's not calculation — it's blooming first
When the light is on, the moth will come
When the fragrance rises, the bee will find its way

[Pre-Chorus]
It's not that I don't love
It's that loving myself comes first
It's not coldness
It's that ease is the real attraction

[Chorus]
A blooming flower never chases the bee
The bee finds the flower on its own
A steady core needs no clinging
Welcome what comes, release what leaves
Give yourself back to yourself
Only then can you give real love to someone else

[Bridge]
The more you fear losing, the easier it slips away
The tighter you hold, the more it shatters like sand
Something whispers quietly in your ear
The less you need, the more people draw near
Ease is the real attraction
Blooming is the real magnetic field

[Final Chorus]
A blooming flower never chases the bee
The bee finds the flower on its own
A steady core needs no clinging
Welcome what comes, release what leaves
Give yourself back to yourself
Only then can you give real love to someone else

[Outro]
I don't need you to love me
I just need to bloom
Quietly blooming in the wind
(fade out)
```
