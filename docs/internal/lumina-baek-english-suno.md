## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(dramatic piano, low and building)

[Verse 1]
I climbed up borrowing someone else's strength
But when that strength crumbled, so did I
Living hidden behind a flashy backing
In the end I was left there all alone
Leaning on a big name, a high position
The day that person left — I wasn't there
When the backing disappears, so do I
Was that ever really backing at all

[Verse 2]
The real backing isn't out there somewhere
There's one lamp inside me that won't go out
A light no one can ever take away
That was my real backing all along
The strength to rise again after falling
A flame no one can ever put out
Even when the whole world turns away
Believing in myself — that's the backing

[Chorus]
Backing is backing — but it isn't backing
The more you lean, the more it eats you hollow
Not seeking anything — that's the great seeking
Finding it inside — that's the real backing

[Bridge]
Money becomes backing, people become backing
Power becomes backing in this world
But what remains the day it all disappears
You only find out when that day comes
Eighteen years of loss before I learned
As long as I don't abandon myself
No one in this world can abandon me
That was the real backing all along

[Final Chorus]
Backing is backing — but it isn't backing
The more you lean, the more it eats you hollow
The seeking that doesn't seek — that's the great seeking
Finding it inside — that's the real backing

[Outro]
Be a lamp unto yourself — 自燈明
The lamp within — I am my own backing
(fade out)
```
