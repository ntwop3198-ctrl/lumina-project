## Lyrics (Suno · v1 final)

```
[Intro]
(energetic drum beat, punchy brass)

[Verse 1]
Tried to hang a pull-up bar on the office door
Turns out I needed screws — didn't know that before
Bought it cheap and thought it would be easy
Bought a hammer, bought some nails, wallet getting queasy

[Verse 2]
Forget it — slapped on some super glue instead
Hung on it once and boom — it hit the floor dead
Tried to take the easy way and almost paid the price
Turns out this pull-up bar was giving me advice

[Chorus]
Force it on and it will fall in the end
Build the foundation right to hold your weight
Even tightening one screw takes care and attention
Live today with that same solid dedication

[Verse 3]
I'm not buying a drill just for one screw
Borrow what you need — that's the wise thing to do
Want to own it all and you'll drown in stuff
Simple and steady — my own path is enough

[Bridge - Philosophy]
Seek the small — start with one screw done right
Even hanging a bar has its order and its time
Seek hardship — fall down, bump around
That's how your bones get harder, stronger, sound
Seek within — in the end it's your will
That lifts this body up and pulls it still
Pull yourself up — with your own strength and skill

[Final Chorus]
Force it on and it will fall in the end
Build the foundation right to hold your weight
Even tightening one screw takes care and attention
Live today with that same solid dedication

[Outro]
Got one done today
Foundation's solid — nothing to fear
Simple and steady
Let's live well
(fade out)
```
