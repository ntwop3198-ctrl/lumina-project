## Lyrics (Suno · v1 최종)

```
[Intro]
(lonely acoustic guitar, calm night stillness)

[Verse 1]
That alley lined with karaoke bars and motels
Neon signs flickering loud into the dark
A drunk hiker stirring a senseless fight
Police and ambulance sirens cut through the night
By day an ordinary alley street
But after dark it wears a different face
Words left unsaid all through the day
Come bursting out into the shadows

[Verse 2]
In the corner of the square the homeless gather
A fleeting laugh blooms over a glass of soju
Eyes so proud though they have nothing at all
One worn backpack — the whole world is theirs
Someone's son and someone's father once
A lifetime flowing in those quiet eyes
That smile beneath the glittering lights
Maybe the most real smile in the world

[Chorus]
Bucheon Station night shows it all
The raw truth hiding behind the neon wall
Whether you have it all or nothing at all
Each one carries pain and keeps living on

[Verse 3]
When the drink goes in the wounds spill out
Pain turns to fists swinging through the air
Sit alone, empty yourself like a hollow boat
Pain and fights drift by like a river flows
The more you try to fill, the more the hurt overflows
The more you empty out, the lighter life becomes
Bucheon Station night already knows
It takes it all in without a word

[Bridge]
Judge and homeless man — the same tonight
Equal before a bitter glass of drink
Daytime titles, daytime faces — set aside
Just human beings sitting in this night

[Final Chorus]
Bucheon Station night shows it all
The raw truth hiding behind the neon wall
Whether you have it all or nothing at all
Each one carries pain and keeps living on

[Outro]
Tonight sit alone
Empty out like a hollow boat
Tomorrow is another new day
Just like that — it all flows on
(fade out)
```
